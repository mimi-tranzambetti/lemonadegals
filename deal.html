Summary

Lemonade Gals is a design agency determined to put a lighthearted spin on tough situations. With the turbulent political climate, the Lemonade Gals wanted to express their concerns in a quirky and fun way, while still staying true to the very real problems exposed in the recent election.

Our initial product will limiting our designs to stickers, with the goal of expanding these designs to other items such as pins and on print. Our stickers are simple to manufacture and fall in the trend of customization of personal items. These stickers can be placed anywhere, from laptops to water bottles, to windows.

Stickers were a viable way to showcase our beliefs without being seen as “overly political” and could be transferred onto a wide variety of everyday objects like laptops, water bottles and notebooks.

They are simple to manufacture and can be produced in-house for a very small price. The Lemonade Gals can use the resources around us, like vinyl cutters and printers, combined with materials we invest in, like vinyl, to make.

These stickers were custom designed by each member, narrowed down to a few designs for print through a survey, and were printed on sticker paper and hand cut. They will be sold through promotion on social media and word of mouth, starting with the college campus audience.

In the future, Lemonade Gals hopes to not only expand to other products, but produce higher quality stickers after initial production and continue to create designs that evolve to address current issues.
