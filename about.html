About the Lemonade Gals
 


The Lemonade Gals Team

Lemonade Gals is a design agency determined to put a lighthearted spin on tough situations. With the turbulent political climate, the Lemonade Gals wanted to express their concerns in a quirky and fun way, while still staying true to the very real problems exposed in the recent election. With the recent conflicts in political beliefs, Lemonade Gals decided to take a stand based on their beliefs. People took to social media to express their concerns, but were mocked by peers and family. Our designs serve as a simple way to express feelings without being labeled as “overly political.”

Behind the Scenes

The Lemonade Gals were inspired to take action by making these designs after the outcome of the most recent election. We wanted to take a stand against the bigotry, misogyny and intolerance which the results of the election encouraged from certain groups of people. We wanted to make small reminders, in sticker of form, of our beliefs which we could proudly display to the world.

The stickers start in the mind of the Lemonade Gals, where we take issues which matter to us and think of ways to visually represent the cause. After sketching the designs and recreating them digitally, they undergo a branding process to make sure that all the stickers maintain some degree of consistency. The designs are then transferred to vinyl and die-cut to leave a white score around the general outline. Made with love, they’re ready to be plastered onto laptops, water bottles, windows and more!
