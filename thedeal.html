The Deal

The Lemonade Gals need minimal funding to get off-the-ground because of the in-house production costs. For the $85 preliminary funding round, 12 unique sticker designs were made, initial market tests were conducted to determine which ones would be most viable, and the first print and cut run was seen through to completion.

As for projections, the Lemonade Gals will never overstock designs because we can print on a per-order basis. Since each sticker costs <$0.50 to make, there will be a profit margin of over $1 per sticker if they sell for a $3 price. Designs can be very broad or specific because of the per-order printing. Most of the revenue will come from the more general stickers, but pull some revenue from more specific political designs.
